Journey to explore Agra

Agra is a city on the banks of the Yamuna river in the Indian state of Uttar Pradesh, about 230 kilometres south-east of the national capital New Delhi and 330 km west of the state capital Lucknow.

Located on the banks of River Yamuna in Uttar Pradesh,It is a popular tourist destination as it is home to one of the 7 wonders of the world,the Taj Mahal.It is a sneak peek into the architectural history and legacy of the Mughal empire with two other UNESCO World Heritage Sites.

    Taj Mahal

    It was built by the Mughal Emperor Shah Jahan as a memorial for his third wife, Mumtaz Mahal.

  • Agra

    Fatehpur Sikri

    Fatehpur Sikri is a town in the Agra district and a famous tourist attraction.It was founded in 1571 century by Mughal Emperor Akbar.

  • Agra

    Agra Fort

    The construction of the massive fort of red sandstone by the banks of Yamuna river was started by emperor Akbar.

    Itmad-ud-daula Tomb

    It was built by Queen Noor Jahan for her parents in the middle of the Persian Garden.

    Mehtaba Bagh

    Mehtab Bagh is a rare retreat of natural bliss and one of the most scenic spots in Agra.

  • Agra

    Akbar's Tomb

    The Tomb of Akbar is an important and iconic Mughal architectural masterpiece, built in 1605 and 1613.
